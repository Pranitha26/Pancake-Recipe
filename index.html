Delicious Pancakes!

Learn how to make pancakes!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Plain Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Vanilla Extract

Instruction

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar and baking powder.
  2. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg, oil and very little vanilla extract.
  3. Mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Now, finally for the dressing, we can add chocolate sauce, powdered sugar, maple syrup, honey, butter or berries
